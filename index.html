Crystal Collector

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game!
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the radom number generated at the beginning of the game. You lose if your total score goes above the value of the random number.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you, until you click on it.
  • Each crystals value is shuffled at the end of every game, regardless of win or loss.
